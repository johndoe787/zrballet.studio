Our Studio

At ZR Ballet Studio, our mission is to inspire and nurture a love for ballet in students of all ages and skill levels. We aim to create a supportive environment where individuals can grow, develop their technique, and enjoy the beauty of dance.

Founded in 2022, ZR Ballet Studio offers high-quality ballet training for students of all ages and levels. Specializing in RAD syllabus training, we provide an inspiring environment where students can develop their skills and passion for dance.

Our studio is proud to hold annual RAD ballet examinations and organize ballet concerts at the National Theatre of Namibia, offering our students invaluable performance opportunities.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear to class?

Students should wear proper ballet attire: leotard, ballet tights, and ballet shoes. Hair must be neatly tied back in a bun. Adult beginners can wear comfortable workout clothing.

What age can children start ballet?

Children can start ballet from age 3 in our 'Twinkle Toes' classes, designed for young beginners.

Do I need prior experience to join?

No prior experience is required. We offer classes for all levels, from beginners to advanced dancers.

How do I register for classes?

Contact us via email or phone to receive the registration form and class schedule.

Can I watch my child's class?

Parents are welcome to attend special open classes and performances held throughout the year.
